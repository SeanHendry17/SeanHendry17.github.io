Fun Facts


World Wide Phenomenon

The series is available in 44 different languages, and the first book was a commercial success after its 2007 release.

Best-Selling Series

As of 2020, the series is the fourth best-selling in history, with over 250 million copies sold globally.

Character Orgins

The character Angie was created exclusively for the first movie and was not in the book.

Original Draft

The initial draft of the first book was 1,300 pages long.
