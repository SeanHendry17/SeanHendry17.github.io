Summary

In "Diary of a Wimpy Kid" Book 1, Greg Heffley navigates the challenges of middle school with his best friend Rowley.
As Rowley's popularity begins to rise, Greg attempts to leverage it for his own gain, leading to a series of humorous events that test their friendship.
The book captures the ups and downs of adolescence, showcasing Greg's misadventures and his struggle to fit in.
